CSE 170
Emotional Intelligence Self-Assessment

This assessment is to help you understand how you rate yourself in each of the emotional intelligence categories.

Self-Awareness

Rate how well you understand yourself and your tendencies.

1. I quickly realize when my thoughts turn negative or make me angry.

2. I am guided by my own beliefs and values rather than what others think, say or do.

3. I am open to new ideas and am unafraid of being wrong about something.

4. I can take a tough, principled stand even if it is unpopular or comes at a personal cost.

5. I will challenge unethical actions of others when needed.

6. I respect the time and interests of others.

7. I try to be empathetic with other's feelings during meetings.

Self-Management

Rate how well you are able to manage different aspects of your self.

8. I am open to feedback during discussions with others.

9. I can articulate feelings and emotions appropriately during meetings.

10. I strive to show compassion to others.

11. I can reflect on my actions, learning from my mistakes.

12. I think clearly and stay focused under pressure when I have to make tough decisions.

13. I constantly strive to improve myself.

14. I am willing to change the way of doing things when current methods are not working.

Social Awareness: Empathy

Rate how well you are able to understand your relationships with others.

15. I freely share my thoughts and feelings with others around me.

16. I will challenge bias and intolerance by speaking up when needed.

17. I respect the viewpoints, backgrounds and opinions of others around me.

18. I strive to build a common understanding with others around me.

19. I like to see others around me succeed.

20. I try to help others around me develop their strengths.

21. I am respectful and courteous toward others around me.

Social Awareness: Organizational Awareness

Rate how well you are able to understand relationships within a larger community, such as a church group, work team or extended family.

22. I strive to meet the expectations of the organizations I belong to.

23. I am loyal to organizations I belong to and encourage others to be as well.

24. I fully understand the expected outcomes of decisions made by organizations I belong to.

25. I strive to place the best interests of organizations I belong to over my own self-interests.

26. I come to meetings of organizations I belong to prepared and well informed.

27. I disclose any potential conflicts of interest in decisions made by organizations I belong to.

28. I strive to understand the needs of people in organizations I belong to whose situation is different from mine.

Relationship Management: Influence, Coach and Mentor

Rate how well you are able to manange and affect your relationships with others.

29. I actively seek ways to resolve conflicts with others around me.

30. I try to maintain a positive attitude toward others around me.

31. I communicate honestly during group discussions.

32. I actively participate in group discussions.

33. I try to be an example to others around me.

34. I take my work seriously and encourage others around me to do the same.

35. I help ensure that everyone gets the opportunity to speak during group meetings.

Relationship Management: Teamwork / Leadership

Rate how well you are able to manage and affect relationships within a larger community, such as a church group, work team or extended family.

36. I strive to adhere to the rules and policies of the organizations I belong to.

37. I discourage negative discussions about other people's responsibilities within organizations I belong to.

38. I openly and publicly express the value of my organization membership to other members.

39. I consider my organizations' missions and/or goals when making decisions.

40. I discuss sensitive and confidential information in my organizations only when it's appropriate.

41. I consider the implied risks to the organizations when making decisions.

42. I actively strive to fulfill my organizations' assignments outside of meetings.
