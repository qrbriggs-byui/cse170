CSE 170 Emotional Intelligence Self-Assessment

Self-Awareness

1. I quickly realize when my thoughts turn negative or make me angry.

2. I am guided by my own beliefs and values rather than what others think, say or do.

3. I am open to new ideas.

4. I can take a tough, principled stand even if it is unpopular.

5. I will challenge unethical actions of others when needed.

6. I respect the time and interests of others.

7. I try to be empathetic with other's feelings during meetings.

Self-Discipline

8. I am open to feedback during discussions with others.

9. I can articulate feelings and emotions appropriately during meetings.

10. I strive to show compassion to others.

11. I can reflect on my actions, learning from my mistakes.

12. I think clearly and stay focused under pressure when I have to make tough decisions.

13. I constantly strive to improve myself.

14. I am willing to change the way ofdoing things when current methods are not working.

Social Awareness

15. I freely share my thoughts and feelings with others in my group.

16. I will challenge bias and intolerance by speaking up when needed.

17. I respect the viewpoints, backgrounds and opinions of others in my group.

18. I strive to build rapport with others in my group.

19. I like to see others in my group succeed.

20. I try to help others in my group develop their strengths.

21. I am respectful and courteous toward other group members.

Relationship Management

22. I actively seek ways to resolve conflicts with others in my group.

23. I try to maintain a positive attitude toward other group members.

24. I communicate honestly during group discussions.

25. I actively participate in group discussions.

26. I try to be an example.

27. I take my role seriously and encourage others in the group to do the same.

28. I help ensure that everyone gets the opportunity to speak during group meetings.

Organizational Awareness

29. I strive to meet the expectations of the community members I represent.

30. I am loyal to the community and encourage others to be as well.

31. I fully understand the implications of community decisions.

32. I strive to place the best interests of the community over my self-interests.

33. I come to community meetings prepared and well informed.

34. I disclose any potential conflicts of interest in community decisions.

35. I strive to understand the needs of community members whose situation is different from mine.

Organizational Management

36. I strive to adhere to the rules and policies of the community.

37. I discourage negative discussions about other people's responsibilities.

38. I openly and publicly express the value the community provides to members.

39. I consider the community's mission and/or goals when making decisions.

40. I discuss sensitive and confidential information only when it's appropriate.

41. I consider the implied risks to the community when making decisions.

42. I actively strive to fulfill my community assignments outside of meetings.
